# Student Guide: Login and Teacher Setup

## How to Login

1. **Go to the login page** - Ask your teacher for the URL
2. **Select "Student"** as your role
3. **Choose login method**:
   - **Google Login**: Click "Login with Google" and sign in with your Google account
   - **Telegram Login**: Click "Login with Telegram" and authorize in Telegram

Your account will be created automatically on first login.

## How to Add Teacher Token

After logging in, you need to connect to your teacher:

1. **Go to Settings** (gear icon in the menu)
2. **Find "Teacher Connection" section**
3. **Click "Join Teacher" or "Change Teacher"**
4. **Enter the teacher token** provided by your teacher
5. **Click "Connect" or "Save"**

Ask your teacher for their token if you don't have it.

## That's it!

Once connected, you'll see tasks assigned by your teacher and can start practicing.
